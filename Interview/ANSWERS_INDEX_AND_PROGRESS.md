## 🎓 Interview Tips - Applied to All Answers

### For Behavioral Questions:
- **Situation**: "In my embedded project..."
- **Task**: "I needed to optimize memory..."
- **Action**: "I implemented..."
- **Result**: "This saved..."

### Technical Interview Flow:
1. **Listen** to full question
2. **Clarify** assumptions (especially for open-ended)
3. **Explain** your approach before coding
4. **Code** with comments
5. **Test** with edge cases
6. **Optimize** (time and space)

### From These Answers:
- Use examples as templates for similar questions
- Apply concepts to variations (when question slightly differs)
- Show understanding of tradeoffs (speed vs memory, etc.)
