# Embedded System Design Interview Questions

## Concepts
- What are the steps of designing an embedded system from requirements to deployment?
- Explain trade-offs between cost, power, performance, and size.
- Describe modular design and firmware/software partitioning.

## Theoretical
- How do you handle firmware updates and bootloader design?
- Discuss security considerations (secure boot, encryption, authentication).

## Coding
- Sketch a basic bootloader sequence in C.
- Outline error-handling strategies for sensor data in firmware.

## MAANG-Level
- Design a scalable firmware architecture for millions of IoT devices.
- Discuss OTA (over-the-air) update strategy and rollback safety.
