# Deep Learning Engineer Interview Questions

---

**Q: What is the vanishing gradient problem?**

**A:** Gradients become very small in deep networks, slowing or stopping learning. Solutions: ReLU, batch normalization, residual connections.

---

**Q: Explain the architecture of a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN).**

**A:** CNNs use convolutional layers (filters), pooling layers, and fully connected layers for feature extraction and classification, especially in images.

---

**Q: What is transfer learning?**

**A:** Using a pre-trained model on a new, related task, often with fine-tuning.

---

**Q: How do you prevent overfitting in deep neural networks?**

**A:** Use dropout, data augmentation, regularization, early stopping, and more data.

---

**Q: What is attention mechanism in deep learning?**

**A:** Attention allows models to focus on relevant parts of input, improving performance in sequence tasks (e.g., transformers, NLP).
